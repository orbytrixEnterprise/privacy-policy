Art-Drawing Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This page explains how Art-Drawing collects, uses, and protects your information.

Introduction

Art-Drawing ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and protects your information when using the Art-Drawing mobile application.

The application helps users learn drawing by displaying reference images through a transparent camera overlay so users can draw images easily on paper.

Information We Collect

  • Device information used for analytics and improving performance.
  • User selected images stored locally on the device.
  • Advertising identifiers for personalized ads.

We do not store personal drawings or images on our servers.

Data Storage and Retention

We only store the minimum information required to provide the application services, such as your name and email when signing in with Google or Apple.

User drawings and images remain stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers. We retain account-related information only for as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. These rights may include requesting access to the information we collect, requesting correction of inaccurate information, or requesting deletion of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the email provided in the Contact section of this policy.

How We Use Information

  • To allow users to log in.
  • To improve app functionality.
  • To provide drawing tools and image features.
  • To display personalized advertisements.

Permissions Used

  • Camera – Used for transparent drawing overlay.
  • Photo Library – Select images for drawing.
  • Storage – Save drawings or edited images.
  • Internet – Load ads and remote content.

Third Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Google AdMob
  • Facebook Marketing SDK

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

However, certain information may be processed by trusted third-party service providers such as analytics, authentication, and advertising platforms including Firebase, Google AdMob, and Facebook Marketing tools to support application functionality.

International Data Transfers

Some third-party services used by the application may process data in countries outside of your own. By using the application, you consent to the processing of information in these jurisdictions where privacy laws may differ.

Advertisements

The application displays ads using Google AdMob which may collect device identifiers to show relevant ads.

Subscriptions Premium

The Art-Drawing application offers optional subscription plans that provide an ad-free experience for users.

All drawing images in the application are available to users. The free version of the app may display advertisements during usage. Users who purchase a premium subscription can access all images without advertisements and enjoy an ad-free experience.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under the age of 4.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information, however no internet transmission method is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
